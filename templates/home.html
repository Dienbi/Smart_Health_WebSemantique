About Smart Health

Smart Health is a comprehensive health management platform that combines the power of semantic web technology and artificial intelligence to help you achieve your wellness goals.

Our platform uses advanced RDFS ontology and Apache Fuseki triplestore to organize and analyze your health data, providing you with meaningful insights and personalized recommendations.

  • AI-Powered Natural Language Queries
  • Semantic Web Technology for Data Organization
  • Real-time Health Monitoring
  • Personalized Recommendations
  • Secure and Private Data Storage
